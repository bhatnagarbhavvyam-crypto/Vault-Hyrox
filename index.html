What is Hyrox

THE WORLD'S
BIGGEST
FITNESS RACE

Hyrox is a global fitness race combining 8km of running with 8 functional workout stations. One format, one world record, one finish line — whether you're a first-timer or an elite competitor.

Hyrox Delhi arrives July 24–26, 2026. The Vault Squad will be there — trained, prepared, and representing the gym.
